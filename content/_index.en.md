Documentation from market dialogues, supplier analyses, and pre-commercial procurement processes related to the SAMT-BU project.

## Why open market dialogue?

SAMT-BU addresses complex, cross-sectoral challenges where solutions are not predetermined. To make well-informed decisions about needs, architecture, and implementation, we need early and broad contact with the market – suppliers, professional communities, researchers, and other public actors with experience from similar challenges.

We conduct this dialogue through open, version-controlled documents rather than closed meetings and email. This provides:

- **Equal treatment in practice** – all contributions are visible to everyone, simultaneously
- **Traceability** – every submission and every change has a timestamp and author
- **Broader participation** – the barrier to contribute is low, and geography matters less
- **Reuse** – the documentation lives on as learning for other projects

## Status

We are in an early phase, testing the method. Feedback on the process itself is as welcome as substantive input on the topics at hand.
